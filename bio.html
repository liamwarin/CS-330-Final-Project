About Me

Hailing from Washington, D.C., Liam Warin is a 21-year-old Chicago-based electronic music DJ and producer. At the age of 19 in his first year at Northwestern University immediately following the COVID-19 pandemic, Liam began exploring the Chicago underground house scene, a primary influence in his current sound. He soon fell in love with the joy expressed in these events and started his own venture into the industry.

Drawing inspiration from classic Chicago house, 90s rave, and modern popular sounds alike, Liamâ€™s DJ sets span across genres, seamlessly blending in 4/4 beats with vocals that are sure to have the audience singing. A journey like no other, Liam seeks to share the same passion, enthusiasm, and sheer energy that he brings to the decks with the audience, immersing the crowd into a journey through a mutual love of music.

Liam is also a student at Northwestern University, where he studies Computer Science and Journalism. Liam is particularly interested in the cross section of artiifcial intelligence models with music, and is currently working on training artificial intelligence models to create synth patches on popular VST synth frameworks like Serum and Vital. As a journalist, Liam covers Northwestern sports for InsideNU, the premier Northwestern sports media organization.
